Title: War Paint

Abstract Bokeh Pan from Lesley Gallinat on Vimeo.

When I created this work, I didn't really have a concept in mind. I was focused primarily on learning the ins and outs of using Adobe Premiere Pro and experimenting with transitions. If I had to sum up the theme of this piece, I would have to say it is about Woman Empowerment. Interpretation is at the viewer's own discretion.
